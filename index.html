Studying, tracking, and explaining the impact of artificial intelligence on South Carolina.

The South Carolina Artificial Intelligence Observatory (SCAIO) is a public-interest research initiative focused on the economic, technological, and policy implications of AI across the Palmetto State.

Why SCAIO Exists

Artificial intelligence is rapidly reshaping industries, institutions, and labor markets. Yet in South Carolina, information about AI activity remains fragmented across universities, businesses, and government initiatives.

SCAIO aims to serve as a neutral observatory and research hub dedicated to understanding and documenting how AI is transforming the state’s economy, workforce, and public institutions.

Core Functions

Research & White Papers

State-specific studies examining the economic, workforce, and institutional impacts of artificial intelligence.

Policy Tracking

Monitoring AI legislation, regulatory developments, and government initiatives affecting South Carolina.

Ecosystem Mapping

A growing directory of AI companies, research labs, startups, and public initiatives operating across the state.

Public Education

Clear explainers, commentary, and analysis helping policymakers, businesses, and citizens understand AI.
